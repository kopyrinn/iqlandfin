About Us

IQ Land is a modern educational platform where innovation meets proven teaching methods. We offer courses and nationwide olympiads in:

  • mental arithmetic
  • English language
  • mathematics

Our team consists of highly qualified teachers and methodologists with many years of experience. We collaborate with leading educational centers across dozens of cities in Kazakhstan and neighboring countries. Our olympiads bring together children, educators, and parents in a shared pursuit of knowledge and growth.
